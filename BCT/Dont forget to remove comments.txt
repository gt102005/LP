------------------------------------------------------------
Gas is the unit that measures how much computational work a 
transaction or smart contract execution needs on the Ethereum 
blockchain.

Whenever you deploy a contract or call a function, 
you must pay GAS.

The payment prevents spam and rewards validators 
who process your transaction.

------------------------------------------------------------
üß† KEY TERMS
------------------------------------------------------------

1. GAS:
   - A unit of computational effort.

2. GAS PRICE:
   - How much ETH (in gwei) you are willing to pay per unit of gas.

3. GAS LIMIT:
   - The maximum amount of gas you‚Äôre willing to spend.

4. TRANSACTION FEE:
   - The actual cost you pay for the transaction.
   - Formula:
        Transaction Fee = Gas Used √ó Gas Price

5. GWEI:
   - 1 gwei = 0.000000001 ETH
   - Used to express gas prices easily.
